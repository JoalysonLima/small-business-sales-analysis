# Dashboard Insights

This dashboard was designed to help a small business quickly understand its sales performance.

## Main Business Questions

- How much revenue did the business generate?
- How many orders were placed?
- What is the average order value?
- Which products and categories perform best?
- Which cities and customers generate the most revenue?
- How much discount is being given?
- Which sales channels are more relevant?

## Key Insights

- The business can monitor net revenue after discounts, which is more useful than gross revenue alone.
- Product and category rankings help identify where to focus stock, campaigns, and promotions.
- Customer rankings can support retention actions.
- City-level performance can guide regional marketing decisions.
- Discount analysis helps evaluate whether promotions may be reducing revenue quality.

## Practical Recommendations

- Prioritise the strongest categories and top revenue products.
- Monitor discounts by category to avoid unnecessary margin loss.
- Use customer rankings to create loyalty or retention campaigns.
- Track monthly sales trends to identify seasonality and weak periods.
